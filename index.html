Species Scroll Library

Juniper Bonsai

Level: White Belt

Sun: Full sun

Water: Water when the top soil begins to dry.

Sensei Tip: Yellowing often means too much water or not enough sun.

Bald Cypress

Level: Green Belt

Sun: Full sun

Water: Loves consistently moist soil.

Sensei Tip: Great for humid southern climates.

Bougainvillea

Level: Blue Belt

Sun: Direct sunlight

Water: Let soil dry slightly between watering.

Sensei Tip: Protect from cold weather.

Japanese Maple

Level: Brown Belt

Sun: Morning sun, afternoon shade

Water: Keep evenly moist, not soggy.

Sensei Tip: Protect from heat scorch.
